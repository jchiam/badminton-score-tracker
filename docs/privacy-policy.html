Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Overview

Badminton Score Tracker is a watchOS app that helps you keep track of badminton scores during a game. Your privacy is important — this app is designed to work entirely on your device with no data collection whatsoever.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No network connections are made
  • No data is shared with third parties

All score data exists only in memory during an active game session and is not persisted after the app is closed.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the developer via the App Store listing.
